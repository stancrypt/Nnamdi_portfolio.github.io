Global Health and Immunization Intelligence Dashboard

This project explores the relationship between immunization coverage, mortality rates, socioeconomic conditions, and life expectancy across countries and regions using global public health data. The analysis combines vaccination indicators such as Polio, Hepatitis B, and Diphtheria coverage with health and economic metrics including infant mortality, HIV incidence, GDP per capita, schooling, and life expectancy to identify patterns, disparities, and high-risk populations. Using SQL Server, Python, and Power BI, the project develops an interactive public health intelligence dashboard designed to support data-driven decision-making, risk monitoring, and health outcome analysis. The findings highlight how healthcare access, vaccination performance, and socioeconomic development contribute to overall population health and survival outcomes.
